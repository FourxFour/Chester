Rationale

Designed as a final project for BCIT's COMP 1950 to display lecture contents. Simplicity, interaction, orization, and cohesion are our goals in this project

Lecture

Designed as a final project for BCIT's COMP 1950 to display lecture contents. Simplicity, interaction, orization, and cohesion are our goals in this project

Blog

In an effort to improve and facilitate communication between students and teachers of COMP 1950, we have created a Tumblr Blog, allowing students to share and post questions and answers. This is an accesible place to keep users up-to-date on late-breaking news, share interesting articles and post other notices, directly from the lecture page.

Materials

One improvement we hope to accomplish with this website, is creating organized and userfriendly access to all course materials beyond the lectures in one space. This avoids bouncing around trying to work backwards to find it all as it is presented. This includes all downloads, exercises, assignments, readings, and resources pursuant to the course.

Design

We wanted to present each page and all of the course information from lectures to resources and exercises in our own unique way but with a design cohesive to that of the BCIT school website. We wanted a straightforward layout with a very clean look using simple graphics and images. We took our inspiration from some of our favourite sites: apple.com, nait.ca, simplebits.com, and alistapart.com.
